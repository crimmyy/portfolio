POV: Bedbug in Medieval Times Themed Bouncy House

Media: Video

Context: 60-120 Digital Media; Golan Levin; s2023

Description: 1920x1080p

Materials: Unity Engine

I found myself looking up from the floor like a bug and struggling to sleep due to my bedtime coffee. As for medieval? My hygiene was barbaric last week. This led me to create a game that puts the player in the perspective of a bedbug in a bouncy house, taking place in Medieval Times.
